Model framework and solutions

Article structure:

  • Analyzed the staffing of airport security systems in different regions, and found that the staffing ratio has a great impact on the flow of passengers
    • It is found from the data that the flow of passengers is S-shaped, and configuration problems are prone to occur in emergency situations
    • Then we built the algorithm to simulate the actual situation and performed the bottleneck analysis (bottleneck analysis)
  • Based on the M/M/C and M/D/C models in queuing theory, we propose a multi-stage flow model, and we build a statistical model to simulate when the ratio of machine detection and manual detection is 7:10 and At 9:10, the passenger flow at the peak and stop period of the flow of people, and use this to establish the optimal length of the conveyor belt
    • Poisson Queuing Equation
    • Computer simulation

      • Algorithm idea: First, use MATLAB to give a list of vectors that satisfy the Poisson distribution to represent the number of people arriving at the airport at time t, and then set the number of machine detection and manual detection as m and p respectively, and then use for and if statements to judge Which window should the person who reaches time t choose, and then accumulate with T, calculate the sum of the time spent in each window until there are no people, and use this to simulate the total time spent in queuing at a fixed ratio; finally, give m and p With the for statement, for example, from 1:10 respectively, the corresponding optimal ratio can be obtained.

      • After getting the optimal ratio, the optimal length of the conveyor belt can be obtained by multiplying the speed of the conveyor belt by the optimal length at which the team starts to detect

  • Use Monte Carlo sampling to analyze the sensitivity of multistage flow models
    • Then we consider the application of the queue length factor in the multi-stage flow model under different cultural backgrounds, and introduce this factor into the model to solve the corresponding optimization problem
  • Under these models, passenger throughput and safety factor can be improved without increasing the number of security guards
    • But our data is not perfect, the real airport environment is very complex and changeable, our model is only suitable for some specific situations

Innovation

  • Multi-stage flow model, combining M/M/C and M/D/C models to get a more realistic mathematical model
  • Computer simulation to obtain the ratio of the optimal number of machines to the number of labor
