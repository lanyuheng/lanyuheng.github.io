Coursework

My supervisor is Professor Guo Changyu from Shandong University, and my co-training supervisor is Researcher Zhang Zhitao from the Institute of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

During my time at school, because my two tutors were teachers of basic mathematics, while I was learning financial mathematics, I also learned a lot of pure mathematics courses, especially postgraduate courses in PDE, variational method and geometric analysis.
